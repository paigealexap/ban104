Welcome to Blazerette

Blazerette is an affordable retail business that specializes in work attire for women. From blazers to loafers, Blazerette has it all.

Mission Statement

Creating affordable professional attire for women entering the workforce.

Ensure that women have the proper attire at a friendly budget since professional attire tends to get pricey.

We connect with college students by hosting pop-ups on campus to promote our company & network with potential clientele.

Thanks for shopping with us!
